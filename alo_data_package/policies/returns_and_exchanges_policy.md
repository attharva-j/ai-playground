# ALO Returns, Exchanges & Refund Policy

**Document Version:** 2025-Q1  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Applies To:** All purchases made at aloyoga.com and Alo retail stores in the United States

---

## 1. Standard Return Policy

Alo will gladly accept returns on unworn, unwashed, unaltered, and unused items in sellable condition **within 30 days of the original purchase date** for a full refund to the original form of payment.

### 1.1 Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a return, all of the following must be true:

- The item is **unworn, unwashed, and unaltered**
- Original tags are **still attached**
- The item is in **sellable condition** (no damage, no odor, no signs of use)
- The return is initiated **within 30 days of purchase**
- A valid **order number or proof of purchase** is provided

### 1.2 Items That Are NOT Eligible for Return

The following items are **final sale** and cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances:

- Items **discounted 30% or more** (including items purchased during sale events such as the Aloversary Sale, Black Friday, or Cyber Monday)
- **Bodysuits and bodysuits with built-in shorts**
- **Underwear and intimates**
- **Supplements and wellness consumables**
- **Café items and food products**
- **Gift cards**
- Items **marked "Final Sale"** at time of purchase
- Items from **third-party sellers** (not purchased directly from aloyoga.com or Alo retail stores)

> **Note:** If an item shows "Final Sale" at checkout, the return will be declined regardless of the reason for return.

---

## 2. How to Initiate a Return

### 2.1 Online Purchases (U.S.)

1. Visit the **Alo Returns Portal** at [returns.aloyoga.com](https://returns.aloyoga.com)
2. Enter your **order number** and the **email address** used at checkout
3. Select the item(s) you wish to return and the reason
4. A **prepaid UPS return label** will be generated at no cost to you
5. Pack the item securely (reusing the original Alo packaging is encouraged)
6. Drop the package at any **UPS Access Point or UPS Store**
7. You will receive an email confirmation once the return is received and processed

**Return Label Validity:** Return labels expire 21 days after generation. Items not dropped off within 21 days will not be accepted.

### 2.2 In-Store Purchases

- Bring the item and the **original sales receipt** to the **same Alo store where you made the purchase**
- You **cannot return an in-store purchase to a different Alo store or online**
- For cash transactions: refunds are issued to an **Alo gift card** (not cash)
- **Split payment orders** (part gift card, part other payment) are refunded to the gift card first; any remaining balance is returned to the other original payment method

---

## 3. Refund Processing

- Refunds are processed **within 2–4 business days** of the warehouse receiving your returned item
- You will be notified by email once your refund has been issued
- Refunds are returned to the **original form of payment** (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional **5–10 business days** for the refund to appear on your statement
- Alo is **not responsible for lost return packages**. We recommend using the provided prepaid label, which includes tracking. If you use your own carrier, use a method with tracking.

---

## 4. Exchanges

Alo does **not currently offer direct exchanges** for online purchases. To get a different size or style, you must:

1. Initiate a return for the original item (following the process in Section 2)
2. Place a new order for the replacement item

### 4.1 Instant Exchange Option

During the return process on the Returns Portal, you may be offered an **Instant Exchange** option:

- Select a replacement item from aloyoga.com before shipping back your return
- A **hold** will be placed on your credit card for the value of the new item
- Your new order ships immediately — you don't wait for the return to be received
- Once your return is **dropped off at UPS**, the hold is released
- If you do **not** drop off the return within **21 days**, your card will be charged the full amount of the replacement item

### 4.2 In-Store Exchanges

- Exchanges for a **different size of the same item** may be processed in-store, subject to inventory availability
- Exchanges for a **different item** are processed as a return + new purchase

---

## 5. International Returns

- Alo does **not provide prepaid return labels** for international orders
- International customers are responsible for all return shipping costs
- Alo is **not responsible for lost international returns**
- Returns must be dropped off within the 30-day return window from the purchase date, accounting for international shipping time
- For international return inquiries, contact: **customerservice@aloyoga.com**

---

## 6. Final Sale Policy

Any item marked **"Final Sale"** or discounted **30% or more** at the time of purchase is non-returnable and non-exchangeable. This applies to:

- Items bought during the **Aloversary Sale** (typically late April, 30% off sitewide)
- Items bought during **Black Friday / Cyber Monday** (typically 30% off sitewide)
- Items in the **permanent Sale section** on aloyoga.com that are discounted 30% or more
- Items purchased with **promo codes** that result in a discount of 30% or more

> **Important:** Alo Access loyalty reward redemptions are **not eligible during active sale periods**.

---

## 7. Defective or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

- Contact **customerservice@aloyoga.com** or call **(855) 793-3100** within **30 days of receipt**
- Include your order number, a description of the defect, and photos
- Alo will cover return shipping and issue a full refund or replacement at no cost to you
- Defective items that are reported outside the 30-day window will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed to be accepted

---

## 8. Order Cancellations and Modifications

- Orders may be **cancelled or modified within 1 hour** of placement by contacting customer service
- Once an order has been **processed or shipped**, it cannot be cancelled or modified
- If your order has already shipped, initiate a return once it is delivered
