# ALO Access — Loyalty & Rewards Program

**Document Version:** 2025-Q1  
**Program Name:** Alo Access  
**Launch:** 2022 (ongoing)  
**Applies To:** All registered Alo accounts (U.S. only for full benefits; international members may earn points but some benefits are U.S.-only)

---

## 1. Program Overview

Alo Access is Alo Yoga's free membership and loyalty program. Members earn points on every purchase made at aloyoga.com or at Alo retail stores in the United States. Points unlock exclusive benefits, rewards, and tier upgrades.

**Tagline:** Shop. Earn. Access.

---

## 2. How to Join

- Alo Access membership is **free** to join — no purchase required
- Create an account at **aloyoga.com** or download the Alo app
- Members who had existing Alo accounts prior to 2023 received retroactive points for all 2022 purchases upon program launch
- Students can verify student status through **Student Beans** for an additional **15% discount** on top of standard Access benefits (one-time use per student account)

---

## 3. Earning Points

### 3.1 Base Earning Rate

**1 point for every $1 USD spent**

Points are earned on:
- All full-price purchases at aloyoga.com
- All full-price in-store purchases at Alo U.S. retail locations
- App purchases at aloyoga.com/app

Points are **NOT earned on:**
- Sale or discounted items (items discounted 30% or more)
- Gift card purchases
- Shipping fees and taxes
- Returns (points from returned orders are reversed)

### 3.2 Bonus Point Events

Alo periodically runs bonus point events (e.g., "Double Points Days") which are communicated via email and the Alo app. These are limited-time events and are not guaranteed to recur.

---

## 4. Membership Tiers

Tiers are based on **cumulative points earned in a rolling 12-month period**.

| Tier | Points Required (12-month rolling) | Status |
|------|-----------------------------------|----|
| **VIP** | 1 – 299 points | Entry tier |
| **A-List** | 300 – 999 points | Mid tier |
| **All Access** | 1,000+ points | Top tier |

### 4.1 Tier Qualification Notes

- Tier status is evaluated on a **rolling 12-month basis** — points from more than 12 months ago do not count toward tier qualification
- If your 12-month spend drops below the tier threshold, your tier will be downgraded at the next quarterly review
- Points accrued across both online and in-store purchases count toward tier status
- Tier downgrades occur **quarterly** (not immediately when points drop below threshold)

---

## 5. Tier Benefits

### VIP (1–299 points)
- Welcome gift upon joining (subject to availability)
- Birthday gift (redeemable within 30 days of your birthday)
- Access to Alo Wellness digital platform
- Exclusive members-only sales (early access to select promotions)
- 10% off first order after joining
- Free standard shipping on all U.S. orders

### A-List (300–999 points)
- All VIP benefits, plus:
- **Free 2-day shipping** on all U.S. orders
- Priority customer service
- Early access to new product drops (before general public)
- Exclusive A-List sales (deeper discounts than VIP-only events)
- Invite to exclusive Alo events (fitness classes, pop-ups)

### All Access (1,000+ points)
- All A-List benefits, plus:
- Free 2-day shipping + **priority processing**
- **Invite-only experiences** (exclusive events, studio sessions)
- Access to private Alo Instagram content
- First access to limited edition drops and collaborations
- Exclusive All Access gifts (varies by season)

## 6. Redeeming Points

### 6.1 How to Redeem

- Points can be redeemed for **free products** from the Alo Rewards catalog, visible in your account dashboard
- Reward products are a curated selection from aloyoga.com and may include apparel, accessories, and wellness items
- To redeem: log in to your account, browse the rewards section, and add a reward to your cart using the "Get Reward" button

### 6.2 Redemption Restrictions

- **Points cannot be redeemed during active sale periods** (including Aloversary, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and any sitewide promotional discount events)
- Points cannot be applied as a dollar discount on purchases — they can only be redeemed for specific reward products
- Points cannot be combined with promotional discount codes
- Reward products are subject to availability and may sell out; Alo does not guarantee availability of specific reward items
- Color and size availability on reward products may be limited

### 6.3 Point Expiration

- Points expire **24 months** after the date they were earned
- You will receive an email reminder 60 days before your earliest-earned points expire
- Expired points cannot be reinstated
