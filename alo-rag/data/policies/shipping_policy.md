# ALO Shipping & Order Fulfillment Policy

**Document Version:** 2025-Q1  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Applies To:** All orders placed at aloyoga.com

---

## 1. Order Processing

All orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. Orders are typically **dispatched within 1–2 business days** of placement.

**Order Cut-Off Time:** Orders placed before **2:00 PM PT** on a business day are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM PT or on weekends/holidays begin processing the next business day.

During **peak promotional periods** (e.g., Aloversary Sale, Black Friday, new product drops), processing times may extend to **3–5 business days** due to order volume.

---

## 2. U.S. Shipping Options

### 2.1 Standard Shipping — FREE on all U.S. orders

- **Cost:** Free on all U.S. orders, no minimum order value required
- **Estimated Delivery:** 3–5 business days after shipment
- **Carrier:** UPS Ground or USPS, depending on destination

### 2.2 Two-Day Shipping

- **Cost:** $15.00
- **Estimated Delivery:** 2 business days after shipment
- **Available for:** All U.S. addresses (excluding PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses)
- **Note:** Two-day delivery is from the day of shipment, not from the day of order. A same-day dispatch is only guaranteed for orders placed before the daily cutoff time.

### 2.3 Overnight / Next-Day Shipping

- **Cost:** $25.00
- **Estimated Delivery:** 1 business day after shipment
- **Available for:** All U.S. addresses (excluding PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses)
- **Order Cut-Off:** Must be placed before 12:00 PM PT to guarantee same-day dispatch

### 2.4 Same-Day Delivery (Select Markets — Limited Time)

- **Cost:** Free (when available)
- **Available in:** Select ZIP codes in Los Angeles, New York, and Miami
- **Eligibility:** Shown at checkout based on delivery address
- **Order cut-off:** Orders must be placed before 12:00 PM PT for same-day delivery
- **Note:** Same-day delivery is fulfilled by a third-party courier and is subject to availability. Alo cannot guarantee same-day delivery during peak periods.

### 2.5 ALO Access Member Shipping Perks

| Tier | Shipping Perk |
|------|--------------|
| VIP (1–299 pts) | Free standard shipping |
| A-List (300–999 pts) | Free 2-day shipping on all orders |
| All Access (1,000+ pts) | Free 2-day shipping + priority processing |

## 3. International Shipping

### 3.1 Free International Shipping Threshold

- **Free shipping** on international orders over **$75 USD** (before taxes and duties)
- Orders under $75 USD: shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on destination
- International shipping is fulfilled via DHL or a local courier partner

### 3.2 International Delivery Estimates

| Region | Estimated Delivery |
|--------|-------------------|
| Canada | 5–8 business days |
| United Kingdom | 7–10 business days |
| European Union | 7–12 business days |
| Australia / New Zealand | 10–14 business days |
| South Korea | 7–10 business days |
| Japan | 7–10 business days |
| Rest of World | 10–20 business days |

*Estimates begin from the date of shipment, not from the date of order.*

### 3.3 Duties, Taxes, and Customs

- Customs duties, import taxes, and VAT are the **responsibility of the customer** and are not included in the product price or shipping cost
- Alo is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing
- If a package is refused at customs and returned to Alo, the customer will be refunded for the product price only — original shipping costs will not be refunded

---

## 4. Order Tracking

- A **shipping confirmation email** with a tracking number is sent when your order leaves the warehouse
- You can track your order via the link in your email or by logging into your Alo account
- If you do not receive a shipping confirmation within **3 business days** of placing your order, contact customer service

---

## 5. Address Accuracy

- Alo is **not responsible for delays, losses, or additional costs** resulting from an incorrect or incomplete shipping address provided at checkout
- If you need to update your address, contact customer service **within 1 hour** of placing your order. Address changes cannot be guaranteed after an order is processed.
- Orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer are not eligible for reshipping or refund if the package is delivered or lost

---

## 6. Lost or Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or appears to be lost in transit:

1. Contact **customerservice@aloyoga.com** or call **(855) 793-3100**
2. Include your **order number**, a description of the issue, and photos if the item arrived damaged
3. Alo will work with the carrier to investigate and resolve the issue
4. Resolution timelines vary by carrier investigation: typically 5–10 business days for U.S. orders, longer for international

**Note:** Packages marked "Delivered" by the carrier but not received should be reported within **7 days of the delivery date**. Reports submitted after 7 days may not be eligible for reshipping or refund.

---

## 7. PO Boxes and APO/FPO Addresses

- **Standard shipping only** is available for PO Boxes and APO/FPO addresses
- Expedited (2-day and overnight) shipping is not available for these address types
- Delivery timelines to APO/FPO addresses may be extended

---

## 8. Product Restocking and Availability

- Alo releases new products and restocks popular items regularly
- **New drops** typically occur on **Tuesdays and Thursdays**
- Core colors (Black, White, Navy) in best-selling styles are restocked on a rolling basis
- Limited edition and seasonal colors may sell out permanently and not be restocked
- To be notified when an out-of-stock item returns, click "Notify Me" on the product page and enter your email
