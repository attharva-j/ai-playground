# ALO Promotions & Sale Event Policy

**Document Version:** 2025-Q1  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Applies To:** All promotional events and discount programs at aloyoga.com and Alo retail stores in the United States

---

## 1. Named Sale Events

Alo Yoga runs several named promotional events throughout the year. Each event has specific discount thresholds, eligibility rules, and restrictions.

### 1.1 Aloversary Sale

- **Timing:** Typically held in late April, lasting 5–7 days
- **Discount:** 30% off sitewide on nearly all full-price items
- **Final Sale:** All items purchased during the Aloversary Sale at 30% off are **Final Sale** and cannot be returned or exchanged (see Returns & Exchanges Policy, Section 6)
- **Exclusions:** Gift cards, café items, and select collaboration pieces may be excluded from the sitewide discount

### 1.2 Black Friday Sale

- **Timing:** The Friday following U.S. Thanksgiving, typically extending through the weekend
- **Discount:** 30% off sitewide
- **Final Sale:** All items purchased at 30% off during Black Friday are **Final Sale**
- **Early Access:** Alo Access members at A-List tier and above receive early access beginning the Wednesday before Black Friday

### 1.3 Cyber Monday Sale

- **Timing:** The Monday following Black Friday, lasting 24 hours
- **Discount:** 30% off sitewide (continuation of Black Friday pricing)
- **Final Sale:** All items purchased at 30% off during Cyber Monday are **Final Sale**
- **Online Only:** Cyber Monday pricing applies exclusively to orders placed at aloyoga.com and the Alo mobile app; in-store purchases are not eligible

### 1.4 Flash Sales

- **Timing:** Unannounced, typically lasting 24–48 hours; communicated via email and the Alo app
- **Discount:** Varies by event — typically 20% to 40% off select categories or styles
- **Final Sale:** IF the flash sale discount on an item is **30% or more**, THEN that item is **Final Sale** and cannot be returned or exchanged. ELSE IF the discount is **less than 30%**, THEN standard return eligibility applies (see Returns & Exchanges Policy, Section 1).
- **Frequency:** Flash sales occur approximately 4–6 times per year and are not guaranteed to recur on any schedule

## 2. Discount Stacking Rules

### 2.1 General Stacking Policy

Alo does **not** allow stacking of multiple discounts on a single order unless explicitly stated. Only one promotional discount may be applied per order.

### 2.2 Community Discounts vs. Sale Pricing

IF a customer is eligible for a **community discount** (Military 40%, Healthcare Workers 40%, First Responders 40%, or Students 15%) AND a sitewide sale event is active, THEN the customer must choose one or the other — **community discounts cannot be combined with sale pricing**. The system will apply whichever discount the customer selects at checkout, but not both.

ELSE IF no sitewide sale is active, THEN the community discount applies normally to all eligible full-price items.

> **Note:** Community discounts apply only to full-price items. Items already on sale or in the permanent Sale section are not eligible for an additional community discount.

### 2.3 Promo Codes and Sale Events

- Promotional codes (e.g., seasonal email codes, influencer codes) **cannot be combined** with sitewide sale pricing
- IF a customer attempts to apply a promo code during an active sale event, THEN the promo code will be rejected at checkout and the sale discount will apply instead
- Promo codes that result in a total discount of 30% or more on an item render that item **Final Sale**

### 2.4 Referral Discounts

- The 10% referral discount (see Alo Access Loyalty Program, Section 8) **cannot be stacked** with sitewide sale pricing or community discounts
- Referral discounts may be used on full-price items outside of active sale periods only

## 3. Gift Card and Store Credit Usage During Sales

### 3.1 Gift Card Usage

- Alo gift cards **can be used** during all sale events, including Aloversary, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and Flash Sales
- Gift cards are treated as a payment method, not a discount — they do not affect the sale price or Final Sale status of items
- Gift card balances are applied after all discounts have been calculated

### 3.2 Store Credit Usage

- Store credit issued from previous returns **can be used** during sale events
- Store credit is treated as a payment method and does not affect discount eligibility or Final Sale status
- IF a customer uses store credit to purchase a Final Sale item, THEN that item remains Final Sale and the store credit is non-refundable for that transaction. ELSE IF the item is eligible for return, THEN the refund will be issued back to store credit.

### 3.3 Gift Card Purchases During Sales

- Gift cards themselves are **never discounted** during sale events
- Purchasing a gift card during a sale event does not earn Alo Access loyalty points (see Alo Access Loyalty Program, Section 3.1)

## 4. Loyalty Point Earning During Promotional Events

### 4.1 Points on Sale Purchases

As defined in the Alo Access Loyalty Program (Section 3.1), loyalty points are earned at a rate of **1 point per $1 USD spent on full-price purchases only**. Points are **NOT earned** on items purchased at a discount of 30% or more.

- During the **Aloversary Sale** (30% off sitewide): no points are earned on discounted items
- During **Black Friday / Cyber Monday** (30% off sitewide): no points are earned on discounted items
- During **Flash Sales**: IF the item discount is **30% or more**, THEN no points are earned on that item. ELSE IF the item discount is **less than 30%**, THEN points are earned on the discounted price paid.

### 4.2 Bonus Point Events During Sales

- Alo periodically runs **Bonus Point Events** (e.g., "Double Points Days") as described in the Alo Access Loyalty Program (Section 3.2)
- Bonus Point Events **do not override** the full-price-only earning rule — bonus multipliers apply only to purchases that are already eligible for point earning (i.e., full-price items)
- Bonus Point Events are never scheduled to overlap with sitewide sale events (Aloversary, Black Friday, Cyber Monday)

### 4.3 Point Redemption Restrictions During Sales

As stated in the Alo Access Loyalty Program (Section 6.2), **points cannot be redeemed during active sale periods**. This includes:

- Aloversary Sale
- Black Friday
- Cyber Monday
- Any sitewide promotional discount event
- Flash Sales that apply sitewide discounts

> **Important:** Even if a customer's cart contains only full-price items during an active sale period, point redemption is still blocked for the duration of the event.

## 5. Promotional Event Shipping and Processing

### 5.1 Extended Processing Times

During peak promotional periods, order processing times may extend from the standard 1–2 business days to **3–5 business days** (see Shipping Policy, Section 1).

### 5.2 Shipping Tier Benefits During Sales

Alo Access shipping perks (free standard for VIP, free 2-day for A-List, free 2-day + priority processing for All Access) remain active during all sale events. Tier-based shipping benefits are **not affected** by promotional pricing.
